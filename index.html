ABOUT

The .Astronomy Hacks – Image Gallery is a visual feast of hack day projects from the past eight years of .Astronomy conferences. If you would like your project to appear here, please make sure to upload all relevant details to the .Astronomy Hacks Collector. The Hacks Collector contains the full list of hacks, and was conceived by @eteq, @DainaBouquin, @astrofrog, @becky1505, @zemogle and @drarnakarick at .Astronomy 8.
